## Axiom-Driven Engineering (ADE)

ADE is our foundational methodology. It is an engineering paradigm centered around clear, testable axioms to guide development and ensure robust system design.

To understand the paradigm and how we apply it across our projects, please read our [Axiom-Driven Engineering Documentation](https://github.com/First-ADE/first-ade).
